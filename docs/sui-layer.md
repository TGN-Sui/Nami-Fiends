# Nami Sui Layer v1.0

## Overview

The Sui Layer acts as the ownership and verification foundation of Nami.

---

# Stored On Sui

## Identity

Wallet-bound identity objects.

---

## Passport

Progression anchor objects.

---

## Badges

Achievement ownership.

---

## Guilds

Community ownership structures.

---

## Squad Endorsements

Trust relationship proofs.

---

## Verification

Verification status proofs.

---

## Capability Objects

Examples:
- BoostCap
- SquadCap

---

# Not Stored On Sui

## Messaging

Handled off-chain.

---

## Discovery Ranking

Handled off-chain.

---

## Reputation Calculation

Handled off-chain.

---

## XP Calculation

Handled off-chain.

---

## Notifications

Handled off-chain.

---

## Analytics

Handled off-chain.

---

# Why

Sui should store ownership and proof.

Computation belongs to scalable off-chain systems.

---

# Design Principle

On-Chain = Truth

Off-Chain = Intelligence

SDK = Access

Client = Experience
