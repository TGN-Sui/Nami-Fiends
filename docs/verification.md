# Nami Verification System

## Overview

The Nami Verification System is designed to establish trust while preserving user ownership and privacy.

Verification helps distinguish real humans from bots, sybil accounts, and fraudulent actors.

Verification is separate from Reputation.

Verification is separate from Membership.

A user may be:

* Verified with low reputation
* Unverified with high reputation
* Verified and Elite
* Verified and NPC

Each system serves a different purpose.

---

## Verification Goals

The system aims to:

* Reduce bot activity
* Reduce sybil attacks
* Improve trust signals
* Protect communities
* Enable fair rewards and governance

---

## Verification Levels

### Unverified

Default state.

Characteristics:

* No proof of humanity
* Limited trust score
* Limited protocol benefits

---

### Human Verified

Verified through approved identity sources.

Characteristics:

* Human verification status
* Eligible for Membership upgrades
* Eligible for Boost access

Examples:

* X Verification
* Future identity providers
* Future KYC providers
* Future proof-of-humanity systems

---

### Trusted Verified (Planned)

Higher trust designation for long-term verified users.

Potential requirements:

* Verified identity
* Good reputation standing
* Account age requirements
* Community contribution history

---

## Verification Sources

The protocol may support:

* X Verification
* Email Verification
* Wallet Verification
* Proof of Humanity providers
* Government ID providers (optional)
* Third-party trust networks

---

## Verification Principles

Verification should:

* Respect privacy
* Minimize data collection
* Remain portable
* Remain transparent

Verification should never require users to surrender ownership of their identity.

---

## Membership Relationship

Verification unlocks eligibility for Membership.

Membership does not automatically create Verification.

Verification and Membership are independent systems.

Example:

NPC + Verified = Possible

NPC + Unverified = Possible

Elite + Verified = Required

Elite + Unverified = Not Allowed
