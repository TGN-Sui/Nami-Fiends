# On-Chain Design v1.0 (Sui)

## Purpose

Defines what lives on Sui Move.

---

## On-Chain Objects

### Identity

Owner-bound root object.

---

### Passport

Progression state object.

---

### Badge

Achievement NFT-like object.

---

### Guild

Community ownership object.

---

### SquadEndorsement

Time-bound trust relationship.

---

### Verification

Identity verification proof.

---

### Capability Objects

* BoostCap
* SquadCap

---

## What is NOT on-chain

* XP calculation
* reputation scoring
* discovery ranking
* messaging
* feeds
* engagement analytics

---

## Rule

On-chain = ownership + proof

Off-chain = computation + intelligence
