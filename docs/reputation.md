# Nami Reputation System

## Overview

Reputation measures a player's contribution, participation, and trust within the Nami ecosystem.

Reputation is earned through activity and cannot be purchased.

Reputation is separate from Membership Tier.

A player may have:

* Elite Membership with low reputation
* NPC Membership with high reputation

The two systems serve different purposes.

---

## Reputation Levels

### Newbie (0)

Starting reputation level.

Requirements:

* Default on passport creation

Purpose:

* New users entering the ecosystem

---

### Gamester (1)

Requirements:

* 50+ Badge Points
  OR
* Level 5+

Traits:

* Active participant
* Completed initial onboarding activities

---

### Goblin (2)

Requirements:

* 150+ Badge Points
  OR
* Level 10+

Traits:

* Consistent engagement
* Community contributor

---

### GoonSquad (3)

Requirements:

* 300+ Badge Points
  OR
* Level 20+

Traits:

* Established community member
* Trusted participant

---

### Fiend (4)

Requirements:

* 600+ Badge Points
  OR
* Level 50+

Traits:

* Top-tier community contributor
* High trust score

---

## Reputation Sources

Reputation can be influenced by:

* Experience (XP)
* Level Progression
* Badge Points
* Community Participation
* Future Governance Contributions
* Future Creator Contributions

---

## Design Principles

Reputation cannot be purchased.

Reputation reflects contribution, not spending.

Reputation is intended to be difficult to farm and difficult to fake.

The system is designed to reward long-term participation over short-term activity.
