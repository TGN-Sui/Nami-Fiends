# Nami Identity System v1.0

## Overview

Identity is the root object of the Nami ecosystem.

Identity represents a persistent gaming persona that may span multiple games, communities, and platforms.

---

## Dual Layer Architecture

### Sui Layer

Responsible for:

* Ownership
* Verification
* Badge Credentials
* Passport Anchoring
* Access Rights

This layer prioritizes trust, transparency, and permanence.

---

### Nami Layer

Responsible for:

* Profiles
* Messaging
* Social Activity
* Progression
* Reputation
* Discovery Participation

This layer prioritizes speed, scale, and user experience.

---

## Identity Components

Each Identity contains:

* Unique Identity ID
* Username
* Verification Status
* Membership Status
* Passport
* Reputation Rank
* Badges
* Squad Relationships
* Guild Memberships

---

## Verification States

### Unverified

Basic access.

No boosts.

No squad privileges.

---

### Verified

Human verification completed.

Verification may be obtained through:

* Nami verification
* Approved identity providers
* Verified social account integrations
* Future supported systems

---

## Membership Tiers

### Adventurer

Entry-level membership.

Benefits:

* 1 Weekly Boost
* Basic Premium Features

---

### Pro

Full access membership.

Benefits:

* 6 Weekly Boosts
* 2 Squad Slots
* Full Feature Access

---

### Elite

Premium membership.

Benefits:

* 8 Weekly Boosts
* 5 Squad Slots
* Premium Cosmetics
* Advanced Personalization

---

## Reputation Ranks

Reputation is earned and cannot be purchased.

Ranks:

* Newbie
* Gamester
* Goblin
* Goonie
* Fiend

These ranks reflect long-term participation, contribution, trust, and community impact.

---

## Core Principle

Membership may be purchased.

Verification may be obtained.

Reputation must be earned.
