# Nami Community Guidelines (Draft)

Draft community standards for testnet. Publish on the portal and link from onboarding.

---

## Purpose

Nami is a gaming community platform. These guidelines keep channels, guilds, and global chats welcoming while the owner team scales moderation tooling.

---

## Core rules

1. **Respect people** — No harassment, hate speech, threats, or targeted abuse.
2. **No impersonation** — Do not pretend to be Nami staff, game developers, or other members.
3. **Honest identity** — Passport and nodename claims must reflect you or studios you represent. `@fiend` prefix is reserved for official nodename policy.
4. **Safe content** — No illegal activity, malware links, or explicit exploitation material.
5. **No spam** — Avoid repetitive promo, bot flooding, or scam links (including wallet drainers).
6. **Follow channel rules** — Game and guild owners may set stricter room rules within these bounds.

---

## Enforcement (testnet)

| Action | Who | Effect |
|--------|-----|--------|
| Warning | Official owner | On-record conduct signal |
| Mute / channel ban | Official owner | Reduced chat access |
| Black passport | Official owner | Severe restriction per [conduct-system.md](./conduct-system.md) |
| Appeal | Member | Formal request via recovery/appeals flow |

Official moderators listed in UI are **placeholders** on testnet — only the AdminCap holder executes enforcement until delegated caps ship.

---

## Studios and game channels

```text
Submit games via Enter Nami ticket flow — no false approval claims
Trust Score reflects linked platforms; do not falsify playtime or reviews
Partner banners require officials review before display
```

---

## Gifts, boosts, and payments

When checkout is live:

```text
No chargeback abuse or fraudulent payment testing on real cards
Treasury and membership terms will be posted before paid tiers go live
```

Mock checkout is disabled on official testnet builds.

---

## Reporting

Members should report issues via:

```text
In-app officials suggestion flow (synced to receiving server)
Support email published at launch
```

Include channel name, wallet or display name, and screenshots where helpful.

---

## Owner and team conduct

Nami team members with complimentary Elite access still follow these guidelines. Official owner wallet (`FIEND` label) is identifiable in UI for transparency.

---

## Updates

Guidelines may change during testnet. Material updates should be announced in Nami Hub and noted in [roadmap.md](./roadmap.md) launch checklist.
